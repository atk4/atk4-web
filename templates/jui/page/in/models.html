Introducing Models

Model implementation in Agile Toolkit gives you a great opportunity to bind Views to a business entity. Models, implemented as pure PHP classes, define properties, behaviour, relations and conditions of a business entity. Once defined they can be inherited and used inside any View - Form, Grid, Lister, Auth and so on.

View-level integration

Instead of associating each page with model, Agile Toolkit associates it on a View level. That means you can have multiple elements on your page associated with different models working seamlessly.

Form below actually adds employees to the database:

$form=$page->add('MVCForm'); $form->setModel('Employee',array('name','salary')); $form->addSubmit(); if($form->isSubmitted()){ $form->update(); $form->js()->univ() ->successMessage('Employee added') ->execute(); }
