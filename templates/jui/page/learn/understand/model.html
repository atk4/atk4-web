Models

Models are yet another gem of Agile Toolkit. The implementation of Models in Agile Toolkit is lightweight and very intuitive - and provides an elegant way for you to define your business logic.

If you have used ORMs before, you will probably find Agile Toolkit Models more 'self-sufficient', often incorporating some of the controller logic. The Models in your application are designed to help keep your objects securely separated and organised.

The use of Models in Agile Toolkit is optional, but very highly recommended for anything more sophisticated than a contact form.
