Learning should be fun!

While you are trying to learn Agile Toolkit, let's keep it fun and informal. But first - the good news:

Examples are as easy as they look

Have you seen those blogs which teach you how to do things,but you need to create 5 files and do multiple tweaks, before finally being able to see any result?

Examples in Agile Toolkit documentation are straight forward. They are short and compact. Apart from Agile Toolkit, you don't need to have anything else. If you have gone through the installation documentation, you will know where to put your example code.

Do not hesitate to copy-paste any example into your local installation of Agile Toolkit. You'll find out that button shape, layout or font-size may differ to match the configuration of your own environment, but the result and logic will remain the same
